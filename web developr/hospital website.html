Why Trust Our Rankings

We are comprehensive

U.S. News evaluates nearly 6,000 hospitals each year - more than any other public hospital ranking. All non-military US hospitals are put through a rigorous eligibility process that includes factors such as complexity of care patients require, the number of patients treated and the level of care a hospital is able to provide.

Our data analysis includes more than 67 million Medicare beneficiaries (nearly 1 in 4 Americans) and ~1.5 billion medical claims over a 5-7 year period -- in 12 medical specialties and 20 unique surgical procedures or treatment of medical conditions.

Patients are our #1 concern

Eligible hospitals are assessed on three key areas:
  • Outcomes: how well patients survived or recovered.
  • Process: how well the hospital follows the best standards of care.
  • Structure: hospital resources and technologies.

We collaborate with industry experts

Each year, our health team consults with industry experts to refine and evolve our methodology, increasingly adding objective data to ensure the best-in-class information for people making critical health decisions.

Detailed and transparent methodology

U.S. News is committed to transparency and therefore provides detailed methodology documentation for hospitals as well as an overview for people choosing a hospital. Read more here
